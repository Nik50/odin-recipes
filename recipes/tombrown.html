Tom Brown


Ingredients for Tom Brown

  • Roasted Corn Flour
  • Milk
  • Honey(optional)
  • Salt
  • Nutmeg(optional)
  • Cinnamon Powder
  • A pinch of ginger powder

Things to Note

  • Add more water if the paste is too thick for you
  • Likewise, add more roasted corn flour if the paste is too thin
  • Make sure you stir uniformly to avoid lumps from forming

Instructions to prepare Tom Brown

  1. Pour the roaster corn flour into a clean glass
  2. Add a reasonable amount of water and stir to form a thick paste
  3. Make sure you stir uniformly to avoid lumps from forming
  4. Pour water into a cooking pot and set on fire under high heat
  5. Allow the water to boil and lower the heat
  6. Pour the mixture into the hot water
  7. Stir continuously and uniformly until it thickens up
  8. Add little pinch of salt and nutmeg (optional)
  9. For a spicy taste, add some cinnamon powder to the mixture and stir
  10. If the mixture is too thick for your liking, add water in bits.
  11. Add honey or sugar to taste and stir consistently
  12. Reduce heat and allow the mixture to boil for about 10 minutes
  13. Check if sugar is okay
  14. Put fire off and remove food from fire.
  15. Your Tom Brown is ready for consumption
  16. Serve whiles hot
