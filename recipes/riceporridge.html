Rice Porridge


Ingredients for Rice Porridge

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 5 cups of water
  • A pinch of salt
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk (optional)

What Rice Porridge Can Be Served With

Rice porridge is mostly eaten with bread alone or bread and fried egg for breakfast.

Instructions to prepare Rice Porridge

  1. Pour five cups of water into a saucepan, add pinch of salt and put on fire. (You can add more water depending on the consistency you prefer).
  2. Measure one cup of rice into a bowl and wash.
  3. Drain the water from the rice and add to water on fire.
  4. Stir and leave it to boil till rice soaks water and cooks
  5. When it is finally ready, serve and add sugar and milk as you want it
  6. Serve whiles hot
