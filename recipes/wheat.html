Wheat Porridge


Ingredients for Wheat Porridge

  • 1 cup of whole wheat
  • 6 cups of water
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)

What Wheat Porridge Can Be Served With

Wheat porridge can be served with black berries, strawberries, bread and fried egg

Instructions to prepare Wheat Porridge

  1. Boil two cups of water in a saucepan.
  2. Pour in the wheat.
  3. Cover it with the lid and remove it from the heat source.
  4. Leave it to soak for about six hours. After that, drain the water from the wheat using a colander
  5. Add the wheat soaked early on with the four cups of water left and boil for about 15 minutes. Let it be on medium heat.
  6. After 15 minutes, turn off the fire and wait till the wheat berries open up
  7. Serve whiles hot. You can add sugar and milk to your taste.
