Gari & Beans


Ingredients for Gari & Beans

  • ½ cup of black eyed beans
  • Palm oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 teaspoon of powdered pepper
  • Maggi spice
  • Two tomatoes/li>
  • ½ clove of garlic
  • Small momone
  • ¼ cup of gar

What Gari & Beans Can Be Served With

Gari and beans can be served with ripe plantain, fried or boiled yam, pear, boiled egg or fried fish..

Instructions to prepare Gari & Beans

  1. Soak beans in water for about an hour or till it becomes soft.
  2. Drain the water, add fresh water and bring to boil till it becomes soft. If water around it finishes and it’s still not soft, add more and stir. Leave on fire till it softens.
  3. Remove from fire when it’s ready and set aside.
  4. Chop your onions and tomatoes. Add garlic and a little bit of maggi and blend together with just a little water, enough to blend.
  5. Wash your momone and set aside.
  6. Put another saucepan on fire.
  7. Pour some palm oil into it.
  8. Put another saucepan on fire.
  9. Add your blended tomatoes, onion, garlic and maggi into the palm oil.
  10. Let it cook on fire for a while and stir where necessary
  11. Add a little salt and pepper and stir.
  12. Finally add your cooked beans to it, as much as you want and stir.
  13. Leave it to simmer for a while and remove from fire.
  14. Dish some into a bowl and add some gari to it (the quantity you prefer) and mix
