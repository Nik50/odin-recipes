Oat Porridge


Ingredients for Oat

  • 6 tablespoons of oats
  • Water (depending on preferred consistency)
  • A pinch of salt

What Oats Can Be Served With

Oats can be served with bread and fried egg or bread alone. You can choose to toast the bread with some margarine or just a little oil

Steps to prepare Oat

  1. Put a small saucepan on fire and pour some water into it, depending on your preferred consistency. If you want a flowing consistency, let the water be more and if you want it to be thick, reduce the water.

  3. Leave it to cook for about ten minutes.
  4. When it is ready, pour it into a bowl. Add sugar and milk, garnish with some almonds and serve. The sugar, milk and almonds are optional.
